About EmoAware

EmoAware is an emotional intelligence web platform designed to provide empathetic AI-powered conversations, mood tracking, and emotional insights.

The system integrates Artificial Intelligence with core Data Structures such as Stack (LIFO tracking), HashMap-based analytics, and structured session-based user identification.

Built with a full-stack architecture using JavaScript, MongoDB, and AI APIs, EmoAware aims to make digital spaces feel more human.
